Ceefax Videprinter

A nostalgic look back to the days of Saturday afternoons glued to the Ceefax Football Videprinter.

Lovingly created by Dan Abrey and Patrick Pawsey-Vale, it shows a Ceefax-ified version of the BBC live scores videprinter.

You can find the Videprinter at page 337

(try typing with your remote control keyboard)

Even better in full screen mode! Press F11 on your keyboard, in most browsers.
